Who is TheDominoSwag Jin?

TheDominoSwag Jin (I) is a retired domino YouTuber who builds unique Domino Rally and Bulk Dominoes ASMR videos and #shorts.

I had been creating numerous domino series for classic, modern, and mixed.

Before TheDominoSwag Jin

On October 27, 2007, I joined my first YouTube channel called 'jjm9512.' I have enjoyed watching many ßtoppling domino videos.

So I began to upload my domino videos in May 2009 and started the classic domino series afterward. As a result, I had very few subscribers milestones back then.

During TheDominoSwag Jin

I changed my initial YouTube channel name to 'TheDominoSwag Jin' in December 2012.

Around 2014, I was running out of domino ideas. After watching FlippyCat's video, 'Spectacular Domino Rally Stunt Screen Link,' I was able to invent something unique; therefore, I have expanded types of modern domino series beginning in early 2015.

Frequently Asked Questions

In exact, there were three questions and three answers, respectively.

“Q1. Did you buy more dominoes?”
I instead purchased more domino stunts than dominoes because I utilized domino stunts more when making more domino videos.
“Q2. How many dominoes did you own?”
I had a total of 2,371 dominoes, excluding pivot dominoes, wooden blocks, Kinetic Planks, and Kinetic Building Blocks.
“Q3. What was your past video editing software to edit and your camera(s) to take?”
I had been editing regular domino videos on iMovie since late 2014. And for the domino #Shorts, I edited short domino clips in the YouTube app and the Adobe Premiere Rush app. I used my Samsung Galaxy Note 8 to take photos and videos.
